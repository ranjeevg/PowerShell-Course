- the Get-Service cmdlet is very common
    - another one: the get-process cmdlet

- the Select-Object cmdlet is another helpful one: basically selecting the fields of the object to project

template:
    Get-Service <service name> | Select-Object <[string array of properties to ve exposed, in that order]
    - the '|' is the same as the pipe command on Bash

    An example:

    Given the service VSS, if we only want the service display name and status:

        Get-Service VCC | Select-Object DisplayName, Status

    (we can obviously play around with the properties projected, based on the response model)

        - to view the entire schema, we can usee our old friend the asterisk again:
            <cmdlet> | Select-Object *
